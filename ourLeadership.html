Kenya Scoliosis Foundation

Esther Wangui Gathiru

Founder & President

Esther founded Scoliosis Foundation of Kenya in 2018 and launched it in 2019 while studying clinical psychology, specifically researching Scoliosis and spine deformities in patients. She is also a scoliosis warrior from 2017. She has had the opportunity to present KSF at national and international conferences.

Andrea Lebel

Co-Founder

Andrea Lebel is a PT 35 years and an international instructor in Physiotherapy Scoliosis Specific Exercise (PSSE). She also is operaing and offering Scoliosis Physiotherapy posture and rehabilitation center. She is also a member and the secretary of Sosort.
