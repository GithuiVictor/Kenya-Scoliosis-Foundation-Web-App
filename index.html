What We Do

Help Us

Scoliosis Foundation (KSF) aims to educate, screen and create awareness of Scoliosis in our community. Our goal is treating the condition with bracing, surgery and providing physiotherapy where necessary. In Kenya, as it is in may parts of the world, treating scoliosis is exorbitantly expensive hence making it less accessible to many. KSF aims at reducing the cost of treatment and management by forming a consortium that will work together to ensure that there is awareness of this condition as well as make available the right equipment for treatment.
